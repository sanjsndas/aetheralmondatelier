    Your trust is our foundation. Aether Almond Atelier respects the confidentiality of every visitor, subscriber, and culinary collaborator. This policy explains how we handle personal data, what we never collect, and how you remain in control of your almond journey.

    📜 1. Who We Are & Scope

    Aether Almond Atelier (the “Atelier”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the red-themed culinary archive at www.aetheralmondatelier.com and associated contact portals. Our platform contains over 58,000 original words dedicated to almond gastronomy, crimson kitchen techniques, and recipe alchemy. This privacy policy describes how we gather, use, and safeguard any information provided when you interact with our website, contact form, email correspondence, and social touchpoints.

    🧾 Note: This site is a simulated front-end prototype for demonstration of privacy-first culinary experience. Nonetheless, the values described reflect our real-world commitment: zero unsolicited tracking, minimal data retention, total transparency.

    🍒 2. Information We Collect (Minimal by Design)

    We believe that almond alchemy should not come at the cost of your privacy. Therefore our data collection is severely limited:

    • Voluntary Contact Data – When you send a message via our contact form or email (hello@aetheralmondatelier.com), we may receive your name, email address, subject, and message content. This data is used only to respond to your inquiry and is never sold or shared with third parties.
    • Technical browsing data (limited) – Our website does NOT use invasive analytics or tracking cookies by default. Basic server logs may capture anonymized IP addresses for security (duration 7 days). No cross-site tracking or fingerprinting.
    • Email correspondence – Once you contact us directly, we retain your message thread for customer service and reference, but you can request permanent deletion any time.

    ❌ We DO NOT collect: payment information (no commercial transactions), precise geolocation, browsing history outside our domain, or any sensitive personal data.

    ⚖️ 3. How We Use Your Information (Only for Aether Concierge)

    ✉️ Communication

    Respond to inquiries about recipes, almond techniques, collaborations, privacy requests, and media partnerships. We keep replies warm and detailed.

    📈 Site Improvement

    Aggregated, anonymous data to understand which red-theme recipes inspire most (e.g., total visits count). No individual profiling.

    🔒 Security & Legal

    Protect against fraudulent activity, enforce our Terms of Service, and comply with applicable laws (if legally compelled).

    We will never use your data for automated decision-making, AI training, or marketing without your explicit consent. No newsletters unless you opt-in separately (currently no newsletter tool active).

    🍷 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR & International)

    For visitors from the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, our lawful bases include:

    • Consent: When you voluntarily fill our contact form, you consent to us using your data to respond.
    • Legitimate Interests: Improving our crimson kitchen archive, maintaining website security, and responding to inquiries.
    • Legal Obligation: In rare cases where we must comply with a lawful request.

    You have the right to withdraw consent at any time by contacting privacy@aetheralmondatelier.com. We honor GDPR data portability and right to erasure.

    🌍 5. Data Sharing & Third Parties — Absolute No-Sale Pledge

    We do not sell, rent, trade, or lease your personal information to anyone. Period. The only exceptional scenarios:

    • Service providers who help operate our website (e.g., secure hosting, email platform – all bound by strict confidentiality).
    • If required by law, court order, or to protect the vital interests of the Atelier or our community.

    Our website does not embed third-party trackers, external ad networks, or social media pixels without prior consent. Any links to external platforms (Instagram, Discord, Substack) are governed by their own privacy policies.

    🌟 Almond Guarantee: We have never and will never share your data for marketing or analytics resale. The red theme is built on trust.

    🍪 6. Cookies & Similar Technologies — Light & Ethical

    Aether Almond Atelier uses only strictly necessary cookies to enable basic site functionality (like form input memory not stored beyond session). We do NOT deploy analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or fingerprinting scripts. For complete transparency, our cookie policy (available at /cookies.html) lists every single cookie. You may block all cookies via browser settings; the site remains fully functional — your almond exploration uninterrupted.

    Retention period: Contact form data and email correspondence are stored for as long as needed to address your request and for a reasonable follow-up period (up to 24 months) unless you request earlier deletion. After that, personal information is anonymized or deleted securely.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@aetheralmondatelier.com with the subject line “PRIVACY REQUEST”. We respond within 30 days.

    California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal data we collect, request deletion, and opt-out of any “sale” (we don’t sell). For CCPA requests, please use the same email. We do not discriminate against users who exercise privacy rights.
